## Tips for Best Results

1. **Generate 4 variants** per prompt, pick the cleanest one
2. **Reject any with mangled text** — AI often distorts letterforms. If text looks off, regenerate or pick a textless variant
3. **Icons are easiest** — no text to mess up. Generate these first.
4. **Wordmarks are hardest** — you may need 3-4 attempts to get clean text. Consider generating textless versions and adding text in Figma/Canva instead
5. **Check at small size** — zoom the image down to 64px. If the icon is still readable, it's good.
6. For the HTML files, images go in `/images/` folder, referenced as `images/concept-N-type.png`
